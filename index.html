Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Daily Hearth is a local-only Android app. We take your privacy seriously — here is our complete policy:

Data Collection

Daily Hearth does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All affirmations, schedules, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

No Accounts

Daily Hearth does not require an account and has no login system.

No Analytics

Daily Hearth does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking tools.

Notifications

Daily Hearth uses local scheduled notifications. These are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

No Third Parties

Daily Hearth does not share any data with third parties because no data leaves your device.

Contact

Questions? Email us at dailyhearth.app@gmail.com
